Taj Mahal is the beautiful and most interesting place in India. This is a cultural monument of India, which was built by King Shah Jahan in his memory after the death of his wife (Mumtaz Mahal). It has a lot of greenery, a river bank on the back, a large area in front of a lake and a lawn. It is located in Agra, it is one of the seven wonders of the world. It is the most beautiful building made of white marble, it seems like a paradise of dream. It is made in an attractive way and punished with royal beauty. This is the beauty of the wonderful nature on earth.
